About

I am a Senior IT Systems Engineer based in Fayetteville, Arkansas, with over a decade of experience owning enterprise SaaS platforms, identity systems, and the automation that makes them actually scale.

My deepest work lives at the intersection of Google Workspace, Okta, and Python — building integrations where the admin console stops and engineering begins. I have configured SAML and SCIM from the application side, automated provisioning at scale with GAM, and served as the final escalation tier when the vendor docs run out.

I was an early adopter of AI tools and have seen firsthand how transformative they are for IT operations. AI changed not just how fast I work, but what I think is possible. I bring that same mindset to every system I build.

Outside of work, I co-own Arka Motel in Fayetteville (featured on HGTV's Fixer to Fabulous) and run a modern furniture woodworking shop.
